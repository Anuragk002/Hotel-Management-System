What is Poornima Labrecord?

In Poornima Labrecord students can submit their lab-record marks as well as mark their attendance. Our basic idea behind this project is to develop a portal which will solve the problem of keeping the record of lab-record marks. Students will login after reaching the lab and their attendance will be marked automatically. After evaluation of lab-record the students will upload their marks to portal by their registered student ID. Faculties can login to the portal by their respective IDs where they can choose the lab they teach and verify the marks entered by the students. Head of the department will have the overall accesses to portal.
